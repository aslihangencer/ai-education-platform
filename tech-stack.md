# Technology Stack: Accessible Academy

## Core Framework
- **Next.js 15 (App Router, Turbopack)**: Chosen for its superior performance, accessibility support through Server Components, and rapid iteration with Turbopack.

## Identity & Storage
- **Supabase (Auth/DB/Storage)**: A reliable backend as a service providing managed authentication with built-in Role-Based Access Control and secure storage for PDF files.

## Database Management
- **Prisma ORM**: Provides a type-safe, declarative schema that makes managing complex relations between users and voice profiles efficient and predictable.

## AI & Audio Processing
- **Gemini 1.5 Pro (Generative AI)**: Leverages state-of-the-art multimodal analysis for OCR and hierarchical structure extraction from documents.
- **OpenAI TTS (Speech Generation)**: Delivers natural-sounding, high-fidelity voice output for a premium auditory experience.

## Styling & Interaction
- **Vanilla Tailwind CSS**: Utilized for its utility-first approach while maintaining full control over high-contrast color palettes and screen reader properties.
- **Framer Motion**: Smooth, tactile transitions that enhance the "Big & Simple" UI for low-vision users.
